Biscuits and Gravy

Description

A simple recipe of sausage gravy over biscuits.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of biscuits
  • 1 lb. breakfast sausage
  • 1/4 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2 c. milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and bake biscuits
  2. Crumble and cook sausage in large skillet over medium heat
  3. Stir in flour until dissolved
  4. Gradually add milk
  5. Continue to heat until gravy thickens, adding more milk as necessary
  6. Salt and pepper to taste
  7. Serve over hot biscuits
